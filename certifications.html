Certifications

Earn credentials you can slap on your resume (and LinkedIn). Complete the course + assignment for each track—we'll certify you. No fake badges.

How it works

Pro members earn FKTI certs by finishing the full course and the assignment. Your progress is saved when you're logged in. Meet the bar, get the cert—we don't hand them out for showing up (but we do make the bar fair).

Available certifications

FKTI — Python for Data Science

Shows you can use Python for data analysis: variables, loops, functions, NumPy, Pandas, and basic visualisation.

How to earn: Complete the Python course (Sessions 1–15) and submit the course assignment. (Pro)

FKTI — SQL & PostgreSQL

Validates your ability to write SQL queries, use JOINs, window functions, and work with PostgreSQL.

How to earn: Complete the PostgreSQL course and pass the SQL assignment. (Free / Pro)

FKTI — Data Engineer (Airflow + DBT)

Demonstrates pipeline and transformation skills with Apache Airflow and DBT.

How to earn: Complete Airflow and DBT courses and the pipeline project. (Pro)

FKTI — Data Analyst (Metabase + SQL)

Shows you can build dashboards and reports with Metabase and SQL.

How to earn: Complete Metabase and SQL courses and the dashboard project. (Pro)

FKTI — Data Science Fundamentals

Covers statistics, probability, and the math behind ML used in the Data Science course.

How to earn: Complete the Data Science course and the assigned exercise set. (Pro)

Why certifications matter

LinkedIn and resume gold. They tell employers you didn't just binge the videos—you did the work. Proof of skills, not just proof of WiFi.
