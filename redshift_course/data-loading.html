📥 The COPY Command

COPY is the fastest way to load data into Redshift — 10-100x faster than INSERT.

-- Load CSV from S3
COPY sales
FROM 's3://my-bucket/data/sales/'
IAM_ROLE 'arn:aws:iam::123456:role/RedshiftRole'
FORMAT AS CSV
IGNOREHEADER 1
REGION 'us-east-1';

-- Load Parquet (fastest!)
COPY events
FROM 's3://my-bucket/data/events/'
IAM_ROLE 'arn:aws:iam::123456:role/RedshiftRole'
FORMAT AS PARQUET;

🧒 ELI5: The Moving Truck

INSERT is like carrying boxes one at a time from the truck to your house. COPY is like backing the truck up to the door and unloading everything at once. Always use COPY for bulk loads!
