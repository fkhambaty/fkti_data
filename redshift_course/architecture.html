🏗️ The MPP Architecture

🧒 ELI5: The Restaurant Kitchen

The Leader Node is the head chef — takes the order (SQL query), decides how to prepare it, and assigns tasks. The Compute Nodes are the line cooks — each one prepares their part of the dish simultaneously. Slices are the cutting boards — each node has multiple slices working in parallel.
