Hi, I'm Fakhruddin Khambaty. I built this site to share what I've learned in data engineering, analytics, and data science—in the simplest way possible.

Why This Website Exists

Start learning for free. In a world where AI has created massive confusion — hundreds of institutes, thousands of courses, everyone claiming to be the best — I wanted to cut through the noise and create something genuinely useful. Core courses and tools are free; advanced courses and premium projects are available with a subscription.

These are my own learning notes, turned into a website. I curated these notes during my own journey learning data engineering, data science, and the tools around them. I do not claim to know every bit of every topic — but because of this website, I can always go back to my learnings, refresh my memory, and also teach others. After all, the best way to learn is to teach.

Every concept is explained in the simplest way I know how: databases become filing cabinets, APIs become restaurant waiters, and SQL queries become conversations with your data. If I struggled to understand something, I made sure to explain it here the way I wish someone had explained it to me.

Whether you're switching careers, a student, or just curious about data — I hope these notes help you the way they helped me. One simple explanation at a time.
