Bias-variance tradeoff?

Bias: underfitting (too simple). Variance: overfitting (too complex). Goal: sweet spot via cross-validation, regularization.

2

Supervised vs Unsupervised?

Supervised: labeled data (classification, regression). Unsupervised: unlabeled (clustering, PCA). Semi-supervised: mix.

3

What is regularization?

Prevents overfitting. L1 (Lasso): zeros out features. L2 (Ridge): shrinks all. Elastic Net: combines both.

4

Handling imbalanced data?

SMOTE oversampling, undersampling, class weights, use F1/precision-recall instead of accuracy.

5

Precision vs Recall?

Precision: of predicted positives, how many correct? Recall: of actual positives, how many caught? F1 balances both.
